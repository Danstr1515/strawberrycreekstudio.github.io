Privacy Policy Hemi's Saint Bernard Rescue Mission Strawberry Creek Studio — Dan Straathof Last updated: April 16, 2025 This Privacy Policy describes how Strawberry Creek Studio ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Dan Straathof, handles information in connection with the mobile game Hemi's Saint Bernard Rescue Mission (the "App"). Information We Collect We do not directly collect or store any personal information. The App is a free-to-play game supported by advertising provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information to serve ads, as described below. Advertising (Google AdMob) The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including but not limited to: Device identifiers (e.g. IDFA on iOS) IP address and approximate location Device type, operating system, and settings Ad interaction data (views, clicks) This data is used to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. On iOS, the App will ask for your permission to track via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before any advertising identifier is accessed. You may deny this permission and the App will continue to function normally with non-personalised ads. For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy App Tracking Transparency In accordance with Apple's requirements, the App requests permission before accessing your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). If you choose not to allow tracking, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalised based on your activity across other apps and websites. Data Retention We do not retain any personal data ourselves. Any data collected by AdMob is subject to Google's own data retention policies. Children's Privacy The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information. Your Rights Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Since we do not directly collect personal data, most requests should be directed to Google AdMob. For any other concerns, please contact us directly. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
