Privacy Policy Strawberry Creek Studio — Dan Straathof Last updated: April 20, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how Strawberry Creek Studio ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Dan Straathof, handles information in connection with our mobile applications, including Hemi's Saint Bernard Rescue Mission, Commonplace Calgary, and all future Strawberry Creek Studio apps (collectively, the "Apps"). Information We Collect We do not directly collect or store any personal information. Our Apps are supported by advertising provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information to serve ads, as described below. Advertising (Google AdMob) Our Apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including but not limited to: - Device identifiers (e.g. IDFA on iOS) - IP address and approximate location - Device type, operating system, and settings - Ad interaction data (views, clicks) - Usage data and product interaction This data is used to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. On iOS, the App will ask for your permission to track via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before any advertising identifier is accessed. You may deny this permission and the App will continue to function normally with non-personalised ads. For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy App Tracking Transparency In accordance with Apple's requirements, our Apps request permission before accessing your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). If you choose not to allow tracking, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalised based on your activity across other apps and websites. Business Directory Submissions (Commonplace Apps) When you submit a business listing through our "Add My Business" feature, your submission is handled via Google Forms and stored in Google Sheets. We collect only the business information you voluntarily provide (business name, address, phone, website, category, and your contact details). This information is used solely to review and potentially add the business to our directory. We do not sell or share this information with third parties. Data Retention We do not retain any personal data ourselves. Any data collected by AdMob is subject to Google's own data retention policies. Children's Privacy Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our Apps, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information. Your Rights Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Since we do not directly collect personal data, most requests should be directed to Google AdMob. For any other concerns, please contact us directly. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our Apps after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
